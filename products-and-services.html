Applications

Our Gold Nanorods will redefine the next generation of lateral flow test development.

Gold Nanorods

Sona’s unique gold nanorod technology combines the benefits of multicoloured nanoparticles with the performance and ease of use of traditional spherical gold to power your next generation lateral flow assays.

Conjugation Service

Sona can combine your biologicals with our nanorods to offer a fully optimized conjugate for your assays, reducing your development time and allowing you to focus on test development.

Lateral Flow Test Development

Sona provides a tailored, modular contract development service from proof of concept through to full development and transfer to production, helping you bring your tests to market.

Lateral Flow Test Production

Sona is partnered with world-class, third-party manufacturers to manufacture pilot and validation batches through to large-scale bulk volumes to serve your market.

Lateral Flow Test Reader

The ‘Cube’ is the next generation of test readers – small, compact, user-friendly, intuitive, wireless, cost-effective and provides clear results. It serves as a virtual ‘electronic eye’ to turn qualitative tests into quantitative tests.

Mobile Apps On The Cloud

ePRO (Electronic patient record outcomes) and EDC (Electronic Data Capture) that record test data using a secure cloud-based platform to collect and store results for monitoring, predictive analysis and epidemiology.

Breaking News

Sona partners with GE Healthcare Life Sciences to complete Coronavirus Rapid Screening Test

Halifax, Nova Scotia, March 3, 2020 – Sona Nanotech Inc. (“Sona”) (CSE: SONA) and GE Healthcare Life Sciences will jointly complete test development of the Sona Covid-19 Coronavirus rapid response lateral flow test, and will use GE Healthcare Life Sciences’ Fast Flow High Performance Membrane (FFHP) in production of the test. Sona will retain all commercial rights to the resulting test.
